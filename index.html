About

I am a student at Hunan University (Class of 2022), and I will begin a joint PhD program at Renmin University of China and Zhongguancun in 2026. My research focuses on affective computing — building systems that understand and respond to human emotions using multimodal signals (speech, facial expressions, physiological signals, and text).

Affective Computing Multimodal ML Emotion Recognition

Research Interests

Emotion / Sentiment Recognition
Multimodal fusion of speech, vision and text for robust emotion understanding.
Affective Dialogue Systems
Designing conversational agents that adapt to user emotional state.
Physiological Signal Analysis
Using biosignals to enrich emotion models and personalization.
